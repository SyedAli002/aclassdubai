SCHOOL & COLLEGE BUS

We shuttle school children in all the major cities of the UAE such as Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah and Ajman on a Yearly contract.


One of our main focus areas is the service we provide to various Schools and Universities in the UAE. School transportation makes up for most of our experience in the field of ground transportation and we take pride in the fact that we have sustained several long-lasting relationships with different schools over the years.

Years of Experience

No : 1

Safety

We generally pay careful attention to all our passengers’ safety while transferring them onboard our vehicles but when it comes to School Buses, no amount of close attention is ever considered enough. For this reason, we have always welcomed the RTA’s and DOT’s initiatives to improve the safety of School Buses in the cities of Dubai and Abu Dhabi and also elsewhere in the UAE. The yellow paint on buses that stands out, the bold lettering and decals on the body of the busy, the flashing beacon lights, external cameras onboard, 3-point seat belts for all seats, are all examples of the safety initiatives reflected on our school bus fleet.

No : 2

Security

Using hi-tech equipment such as CCTV cameras with live-streaming feature, GPS live tracking and RFID student tracking, our Logistics team always have answers to the following information at any given point in time:

  • Where are all our school buses located? (GPS trackers)
  • What is happening onboard? (Live streaming CCTV footage)
  • Which student is onboard? (Student RFID trackers)

No : 3

Comfort

We have taken measures to make the ride for our precious passengers as comfortable as possible. Most of our School Buses run on petrol engines rather than the usually preferred diesel engines. Petrol engines are generally less-noisy making the interior cabin as comfortable as possible. Our professionally trained drivers make sure the buses are always clean, well-air conditioned and free off unpleasant odors etc.
